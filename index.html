1.3. Purposes of Processing

  • Providing game services (4-player Turkish Okey "Dayama" variant)
  • Online multiplayer matchmaking (Photon PUN 2)
  • Global leaderboard (Firebase Firestore)
  • Anti-cheat and fraud prevention
  • Profanity filter for usernames
  • Compliance with legal obligations (KVKK, GDPR, Apple/Google policies)
  • Ad delivery and measurement (Google AdMob — subject to ATT/UMP consent)

1.4. No User-to-User Communication

The app does NOT include any chat, messaging, or user-to-user communication. Interaction is limited strictly to in-game tile actions.

1.5. Data Transfer (Domestic and International)

  • Photon Engine (Germany/EU): Anonymous UID and nickname for matchmaking. Within EU, GDPR compliant.
  • Firebase Firestore (EU region): Anonymous UID, nickname, XP for leaderboard. GDPR compliant.
  • Google AdMob (USA — under SCCs): Advertising ID for ad delivery. No personalized data transferred if ATT/UMP declined.
  • Firebase Authentication (EU region): Anonymous identity generation.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Anonymous crash reports.

1.6. Data Security

  • Local device data is encrypted with AES + HMAC (SecurePrefs).
  • Firebase Firestore Security Rules prevent unauthorized access.
  • Photon communication uses encrypted channels.
  • All network traffic uses HTTPS/TLS.

1.7. Your Rights (KVKK Art. 11 / GDPR Art. 15-22)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: Request information about your processed data.
  • Right to rectification: Correct incomplete or inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure: Delete your account (see Account Deletion).
  • Right to object: Apply via gamedayama@gmail.com.
  • Right to complain: File complaints with KVKK Authority (kvkk.gov.tr) or your local EU Data Protection Authority.

Requests are answered within 30 days free of charge.

This app is intended for users 13 years and older. The age limit exists for data protection compliance (KVKK/COPPA/GDPR). The game content itself is suitable for all ages.

2.2. Free App — No Gambling

Dayama Okey is completely free. The app contains:

  • ❌ NO in-app purchases

  • ❌ NO betting or gambling mechanics

  • ❌ NO premium membership
